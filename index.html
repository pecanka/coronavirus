CORONOVIRUS: COVID-19

Jakub Pecanka, PhD (Pecanka Consulting, www.pecanka.net)

A collection of plots showing the current state and the historical progression of the COVID-19 pandemic in select countries around the world.

Based on data from Worldometers, OCDC and mzcr.cz
